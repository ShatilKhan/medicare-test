Keep track of all your medications at any time with us!

Medicare is your app for successful handling of medications, tablets, and pills.
With us, you can keep track of which medications you need to take and when. For older individuals in particular, regular medication intake is often a major challenge. Forgetting to take medications poses a significant risk to those individuals and their surroundings. Choose Medicare now and keep an overview of all relevant medications.

After initially adding a medication relevant to you, a separate MediCard is created for each intake day.
This MediCard includes the name, description, intake day, and the time of day when the medication should be taken.
When it's time to take the medication, the text becomes gray and your MediCard receives the "Take medication now" badge.
With the buttons on the MediCard, you have the option to mark the medication as taken and delete it or adjust the intake.
